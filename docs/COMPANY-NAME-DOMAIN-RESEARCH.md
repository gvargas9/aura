## Domain Strategy Recommendations

### Option A: Secure the "Grupo" Domain (Recommended)
For your corporation name, using "Grupo [Name]" naturally leads to `grupo[name].com` domains, many of which are available. This is the most practical path for names where the base .com is taken.

### Option B: Acquire Premium .com
For names like **NutraVio** (.com on DNS parking, recently registered) or **Purox** (on Afternic/resale), you could potentially acquire the .com through a broker. Expect to pay $2K-$20K+ depending on the domain.

### Option C: Use .co or Compound Domains
Domains like `cibofoods.com`, `nurafoods.com`, or `nuravita.com` are descriptive and professional. The `.co` TLD is also widely accepted for startups.

## Key Takeaways

1. **All 4-letter single-word .com domains are taken** (Cibo, Nura, Vivo, Puro) — these are premium and would cost significant money to acquire.
2. **The "Grupo" prefix opens up many available domains** — grupocibo.com, gruponura.com, gruponutravio.com all appear available.
3. **Compound Latin names have the best availability** — NuraVita, TerraNura, VivaNura, PuroCibo, AlimentaPura all appear unregistered.
4. **NutraVio.com is an interesting opportunity** — recently registered (Feb 2025), parked on DNS parking, expires Feb 2026. Could be acquired cheaply or may expire.
5. **NutriPura.com shows no WHOIS data** — your top starred pick may actually have the .com available, but verify at a registrar since a Brazilian company uses the brand name.
